Glossary

  • Folder Pane - The left side panel that displays the mail tree structure.
  • Message Pane - Window to preview the currently selected message.
  • Spaces Toolbar/Menu - The toolbar at the extreme left of the Thunderbird window. Contains icons for: Inbox, Address Book, Calendar, Tasks, Chat and Settings. The toolbar is usually hidden and accessed via the Spaces Menu at the extreme upper left of the window.
  • Today Pane - The right side panel that displays the Calendar and upcoming events. Will become visisble once a calendar is created.
